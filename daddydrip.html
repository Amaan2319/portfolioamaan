Project Overview

DaddyDrip is a feature-rich e-commerce application designed to provide users with a hassle-free online shopping experience. The platform includes a sticky navbar for easy navigation, dynamic product search, and an intuitive cart management system. It highlights responsive design principles to ensure seamless usage across devices.

Built using modern web development tools, DaddyDrip serves as a testament to my ability to develop scalable, user-friendly, and visually appealing applications.
